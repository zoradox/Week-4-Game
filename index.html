Star Dust Collector

How to play!


  1. You will be given a random number at the start of the game."Star Dust Needed"
  2. There are four stars below.
  3. By clicking on a star, you will add star dust to your total score.
  4. You win the game by matching your star dust to the amount of star dust needed.
  5. You lose the game if the amount of star dust you have goes over the amount of star dust needed.
  6. The value of each star is hidden from you until you click on it.
  7. Each time when the game starts, the game will change the values of each star.
